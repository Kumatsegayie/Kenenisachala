About Me

I'm a young architect from Ethiopia with a focus on minimalism, sustainability, and human-centered design. I work with tools like SketchUp, Revit, and Twinmotion to create visuals that speak clarity and function. I am a highly driven and detail-oriented architecture enthusiast with hands-on experience in small-scale site projects. I recently graduated from a non-governmental architecture program, where I developed strong skills in design, planning, and visualization. I am passionate about creating spaces that are both functional and expressive believing that architecture is not just a structure, but a way of life. I am fluent in four languages: English, Arabic, Afaan Oromo, and Amharic, allowing me to connect with diverse clients and teams. I am continuously exploring new ideas, tools, and technologies to push creative boundaries in architecture.
