About

I’m a young architect from Ethiopia focused on minimalism, sustainability and human-centered design. I develop concepts in SketchUp and Revit, and bring them to life with Lumion/Twinmotion. Fluent in English, Arabic, Afaan Oromo and Amharic, I collaborate smoothly across teams and clients.

I care about clean lines, generous light, and materials that age well. The goal is always the same: calm spaces that serve bold lives.
